CLEANLINESS WALK: 1ST NOVEMBER, 2021

Department of Botany in collaboration with Departmental of Environmental Sciences organized a cleanliness drive walk among students in the University. Students not only participated in the walk but also displayed thoughtful posters conveying strong messages regarding environment and sustainability. Students and teachers picked up waste/ liter discarded in the different ground of university. After the cleanliness drive students and teachers also planted trees in the botanical garden: University of Okara. Organizing Committee Members: Dr. Fahim Arshad, Dr. Syeda Anjum Tahira,Dr. Muhammad Iqbal, Dr. Shafiq U Rehman, Dr. Naila Mukhtar, Dr. Nidaa, Dr. Hira Bashir Ms. Kaneez Fatima, Ms. Shaheena Umbreen, Mr. Asad Raza Anjum, Ms. Nimra Arooj, Mr. Kashif Bashir, Mr. Muhammad Amir, Mr. Dilshad Masih.
