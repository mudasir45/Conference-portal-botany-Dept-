INTERNATIONAL SYMPOSIUM: 10TH NOVEMBER, 2020

On November 10, 2020, the Department of Botany has arranged an International Symposium on Current Trends and Future Perspectives in Botanical Research. This symposium was comprised of blended lectures through webinars as well as on-campus participants. Around 200 online and 250 on-campus participants including faculty and students has joined the event. The event was inaugurated by the Chairperson of the Department of Botany Dr. Fahim Arshad. Honorable registrar Dr. Ghulam Mustafa and Director QEC Dr. Kamran Arshad graced the event by their presence on behalf of worthy Vice Chancellor as chief guests. Keynote and invited speakers shared their research expertise and didactic lectures. Participants thoroughly learnt and enjoyed lectures of keynote speakers Prof. Dr. Douglas R. Cook, (USA) and Dr. M. Ziaul Hoque (Bangladesh).
