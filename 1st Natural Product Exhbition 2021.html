1ST NATURAL PRODUCT EXHIBITION: 30TH MARCH, 2021

Department of Botany in collaboration with Department of Chemistry organized 1st Annual Natural Product Exhibition 2021 on Tuesday, 30th March 2021. The Exhibition was inaugurated by worthy registrar Dr. Muhammad Tahir Khan. The products exhibited in the event were natural and organic based on Plants, Animal and Fungi. Oyster Mushrooms cultivated in the Botany Lab were displayed by Dr. Hira Bashir and her team representing Department of Botany. She also guided visitor students about the nutritional values of mushrooms and how these mushroom can be beneficial to Pakistan economy if commercialized on a large scale. Moreover, students of Dr. Nidaa displayed Jute and seed Art, while Dr. Naila mukhtar and team presented Alovera gel and Anti acne soaps, Orange peel masks and neem soaps, scrubs and qehwas. These all products were natural, organic and ecofriendly as well as economic and easy to process.
