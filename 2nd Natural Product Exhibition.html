2ND NATURAL PRODUCT EXHIBITION: 30TH MARCH 2022

Department of Botany keeping its tradition alive organized 2nd Annual Natural Product Exhibition 2022 on Wednesday, 30th March 2022. The Exhibition was inaugurated by Chairperson Department of Botany Dr.Fahim Arshad, Incharge Communication Studies Mr. Zahid Jamil and lecturer English Department Ms. Neelofer along with Dr. Muhammad Iqbal, Dr. Nidaa and Dr. Hira from Botany Department. More than 50 students participated exhibiting different products in 15 stalls. The products exhibited in the event were natural and organic based on Plants, Animal and Fungi. These all products were natural, organic and ecofriendly as well as economic and easy to process. Technical Review Committee evaluated all the products on basis of their cost, processing, nature and Eco friendliness and positions were announced for five groups.
1. Organic Explorer (Minahil) 1st Department of Botany
2. Rehmans Care Products (Dr. Rafia Rehman) 2nd Department of Chemistry
3. Farms Mushroom (Dr. Hira Bashir) 3rd Department of Botany
Natural Serum (Ghulam Sakina) 3rd Department of Botany
Food Stall (Noman)
3rd Department of Botany Ceremony was concluded with remarks of Chairperson Department of Botany Dr. Fahim Arshad who emphasized that these type of practices are important for extensive learning and there should be more events for better practical guidance of students having great research interests.
