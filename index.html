1st INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE ON RECENT

Introduction

A premier event that brings together a unique and international mix of Plant scientists from academia and industry across the globe to exchange their knowledge, expertise and research innovations to strengthen an elite plant science conference. Natural Product Exhibition will also supplement for this conference, basic purpose of this exhibition is to enhance entrepreneurship capacity of academia to prepare and sell their own products by utilizing different bio-sources like plants, fungi and animals, etc.

Objective

The event will provide a platform to exchange ideas, and develop research linkages and foster collaborations in the field of natural product sciences and industries.

Scope

This will be the best opportunity to reach the largest gathering of participants of plant scientists and industries. World renowned speakers, national industrialists, the advanced techniques, novel developments and updates of plant sciences are hallmarks of this conference. In addition, through the Natural product exhibition a link will be created between academia and industry. Students will learn that how they can deal with commercialization and up gradation of their herbal /organic products. This activity also developed academia and industry collaboration and entrepreneurship among the students.
