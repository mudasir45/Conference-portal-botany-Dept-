MUSHROOM CULTIVATION WORKSHOP: 25TH JUNE, 2021

Department of Botany organized a hands on training workshop on mushroom cultivation techniques for researchers and students of University of Okara. Students from University of Agriculture Faisalabad, Sub-campus Okara also joined this didactic session. Dr. Hira Bashir Assistant Professor (IPFP) from Department of Botany served as chief organizer and resource person for the workshop. In the workshop session trainees learnt about different effective techniques for Oyster and Butten mushroom. Certificates were distributed at the end of the workshop. Ph.D. Scholar Shaheena Umbreen, MPhil Scholar Kaneez Fatima, Nimra Arooj and BS Student Kashif Bashir also rendered their services for the workshop. Chairperson Department of Botany congratulated Dr. Hira Bashir for successful workshop.
